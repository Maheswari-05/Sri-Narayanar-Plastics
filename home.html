Who are we?

Sri Narayanar Plastics specializes in manufacturing and assembling precision thermoplastic components for a wide range of industries, both domestic and international. With a focus on quality and innovation, we excel in converting wood and metal parts into highly durable and efficient engineering plastic components. With a proven track record of delivering over 1500 unique products, we utilize advanced injection molding technologies, including insert and over-molding, to cater to diverse industrial needs. Our expertise spans the processing of various materials, including LLD, LD, HD, PP, Nylon, GF, ABS, HIPS, and more, ensuring flexibility for custom requirements.
